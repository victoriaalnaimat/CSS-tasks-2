ABOUT US

Our experienced instructors are here to help you achieve your language goals


At Hi Five , we understand that learning a new language can be challenging. That's why we provide private online English speaking sessions with our highly qualified instructors. Our platform is designed to give learners the flexibility to choose the number of minutes that best fits their schedule and practice at any time. We are so confident in the quality of our instruction that we offer a free trial session to new students. This allows you to experience our platform and teaching style before committing to a subscription. Our goal is to provide a supportive and engaging learning environment that encourages regular practice and helps our students achieve their language learning goals.
